Funerals

Angie has a strong belief that both weddings and funerals are expressions of love - though of course, the words and emotions may reflect the love differently at a funeral.

You would have heard of a 'Funeral Director' but is a 'Funeral Celebrant' the same thing? The answer to this is 'no'. The Director is the person (usually from a Funeral Company) who takes care of the more practical side of death - taking care of the body, setting.

Angie would be honoured to guide you as you farewell a loved one after their death.

Most importantly, as a Funeral Celebrant, Angie brings her compassion, wisdom and experience when guiding you through the funeral service itself.

Every farewell is different for every individual is unique. Angie will gently guide you, with understanding and care, so that together, we create a service that honours your loved one, and that you will remember with love.

We discuss the Order of Service - aspects such as eulogies and who will present them, family participation, music, readings, poems, prayers, hymns (if appropriate), photo montage, haka, or any other symbolic expressions of love you may desire.

Please note: This is a brief overview of what Angie provides as a Funeral Celebrant, please feel free to call her on the number provided below to speak in more detail.
